# GenShelf AI System Knowledge

## Core Purpose
GenShelf is a comprehensive retail and inventory management system designed for small to medium-sized businesses. It handles Point of Sale (POS), inventory tracking, financial analytics, and customer relationship management.

## Key Modules
- **Inventory**: Tracking products, batches, and low stock thresholds.
- **POS**: Processing transactions and handling payments.
- **Finance**: Tracking revenue, expenses, and profit/loss.
- **Analytics**: Providing business intelligence through daily summaries and top-performer tracking.

## AI Assistant Role
The AI Assistant acts as a business consultant that has live access to this data to provide real-time insights and operational guidance.
